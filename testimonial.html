Brayden Rhoton

Regional Sales Manager

Selling pest control door to door seemed like a funny concept to me when I first learned about it, but it has been one of the hardest but most rewarding things I’ve ever done. I started out selling home security door to door, but pest control has been the most rewarding with the highest likelihood for success for me and others who I have worked with. The amount of experience and income potential you can gain from knocking doors for one summer is unmatched in my opinion, at least for college aged people. Also having the opportunity to network and work shoulder to shoulder with go getters who are determined to get out of the rat race and who refuse to get sucked into the normal 9-5 schedule has helped me grow a ton. I would never take back my experiences and look forward to future growth with Hawx Pest Control

Dallan Jones

Sr. Sales Manager

I had zero interest in sales in general when I got to BYU. I was going to be a doctor because I wanted a secure job, and 3 of my brothers are doctors so I was getting a lot of pressure to do that for a profession. Despite them telling me it would be a waste of time, I decided to go out and sell for a summer just because I had some friends who had made pretty good money. I was very surprised at how much easier it was to sell a pest contract than I thought it would be, and I ended up selling five my first day. I made about $1000 that day, which is as much as my doctor brothers make in a day. It proved to be harder as time went on because being on your A-game all the time is not easy, but I have grown so much from this job. I still go to BYU and am finishing up my bachelor’s degree in Biology, but my career plan has changed. There is more security in the pest industry than even medicine with the way things are going in healthcare. I’ve sold for three years now and lead a team last year, I’m looking at being responsible for 2 or 3 teams this year. I get to make as much money as a doctor and I only work 4 months out of the summer. This year I will make more than any of my brothers, and I’m 23, they’re in their late 30’s and 40’s. Selling pest control door to door with Hawx is a great opportunity that I would have no idea about if I hadn’t decided to try it out that first year. It’s the best return on your time financially that I am aware of to date.
